# Business Rules

## User Management
- Users must have a unique username and email.
- Passwords must be hashed before storage.

## Group Management
- Groups must have a unique name.
- A user can belong to multiple groups.

## Role Management
- Roles must have a unique name.
- A role defines permissions for different actions.

## Task Management
- Tasks must have a title and description.
- Tasks can be assigned to users or groups.
